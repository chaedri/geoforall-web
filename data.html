Note that GRASS GIS locations are provided usually as ZIP files, so you need to uncompress them and put then into your GRASS data directory before using them.

  • Hardin, E., Mitasova, H., Tateosian, L., Overton, M., 2014, GIS-based Analysis of Coastal Lidar Time-Series , Springer Briefs in Computer Science, Springer, New York, 84 p.
    • GRASS GIS location for North Carolina coast (northcarolina_coast_spm.zip) includes North Carolina boundaries in its PERMANENT mapset and a NagsHead_series/ MAPSET with time series of lidar-derived DEMs. The DEMs represent coastal topography along 1km of shoreline at 1 m resolution in the town of Nags Head, NC, next to Jockeys Ridge State Park.
